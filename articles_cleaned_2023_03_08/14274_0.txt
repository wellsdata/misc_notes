HORRIBLE CRIMES
OCCUR IN OKLAHOMA

of the lynching of Wilbur Turner
for the murder of George Cason and the
criminal assault upon Mrs. Cason, negroes
have been warned to keep off the
streets of Muldrow where the lynching
occurred and out of Sallisaw, the county
