Colored Mob Broke Down Jail Doors,
But Missed Their Man.
The jail at Brooklyn, a suburb of
East St. Louis, Mo., Sunday night,
was broken open by a mob of negroes,
whose desire it was to lynch one of
their own color for an attempted assault
on a negro woman earlier in the
day. Their intended victim, however,
