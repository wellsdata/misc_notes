Lexington Grand Jury
Refuses to Indict Mob
LEXINGTON, Ky .. Feb. 27- Declaring
that to return indictments would
further aggravate an already tense situation
, the special grand jury which investigated
the riots here recently in
which a mob attempted to lynch Will
Lockett, negro slayer of Geneva Haru
man, finished its work. The jury re
fused to indict members of the mob, al
though admission was made in the
jury's report that testimony which had
submitted warranted such action.
