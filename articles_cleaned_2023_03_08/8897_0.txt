NEGRO LYNCHED.
Kansas Mob Haugs a Negro Murderer
.
Wichita, Kah., May 28.- Bill Campbell
, a negro, was lynched at Pond
Creek, Okla., by a mob of 400 persons,
who broke down the jail, took him to
the scene of his crime and hanged him
to a telegraph pole. He was hanged
for the fatal shooting of Deputy Sheriff
Smith
eorge.
