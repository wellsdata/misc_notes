ELECTRIC CAR KILLS A BOY.
Motorman Charged With Careless
ness and Mobbed.
Chicago, Ill., Dec. 25.- George Demmark,
7 years of age, was run over and killed
this afternoon by an electric car at Eighteenth
and Throop streets. Within a few
minutes 1,500 people had congregated on
the corner, and Fred Bernier, the motorman
as roughly handled. Bernier was accused
of carelessness, and the frenzied
mob threatened to lynch him. They dragged
him from the car and handled him in
such a manner that he is now nursing
several severe bruises. A riot call was
sounded and patrol wagons filled with
officers rescued Bernier from the hands of
the mob. The Demmark's body was
