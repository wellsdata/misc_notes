Lynch Law.
An attempt to enforce the code of Judge
Lynch was assayed, at Dam No. 5, on the
Canal, on last Friday. It appears that
some money had been stolen, and suspicion
having designated a man named Robert
McCarty as the guilty party, he was
approached upon the subject, but denying
all knowledge of the matter, he was unceremoniously
taken in charge by those
present, a rope placed around his neck
and he was at once strung up to a derrick
and there suffered to hang until life was
almost extinct. He was taken down just
in time to preserve the vital spark
