FELLOW PRISONERS SANG.
" God Be With You" While Turner
Was Being Hanged.
San Antonio, Tex., Dec. 26.- While
a quartet of prisoners led by Tom
Johnson, a negro serving a sentence
for assault to murder, sang" God Be
With You Till We Meet Again. George
H. Turner, a white man, was hanged
for cutting the throat of Mrs Elizabeth
Lynch last March in a dispute over
land deal.
Turner's threats to denounce his en.
emies and create other sensations on
the gallows were not carried out. He
only asked Sheriff Tobin to be sure to
fix the knot so that it would break
his neck.
" I don't want it to aurt me," he said.
There were 250 witnesses to the
hanging among them 25 Texas sheriffs
