LYNCHED SEIBOLDT.
A Brutal Wisconsin Murder Avenged by
a Mob at Darlington.
MILWAUKEE, Sept. 23.- A Darlington,
Wis., special to The Journal says: Anton
Sieboldt, who pounded James Meighan
to death last Wednesday, was taken
from the jail here by a mob and hung
to a tree in the park until dead. This
was the day set for his preliminary examination
and he was brought to town-
at 11 a. m. from Monroe, Greene county,
where he had been taken for safety, a
mob having threatened to lynch him last
Thursday. He was cool and adjusted
the rope about his own neck. The
