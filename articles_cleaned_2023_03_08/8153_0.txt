A NEGRO LYNCHED
Bad Negro Lynched and Burned
By Associated Press to The News.
ROANOKE, Va., Sept. 21. The
negro, Thomas Smith, who assaulted
Mrs. Henry Bishop
last night, was lynched this morn
ing. He was found by the mob
where the authorities had secreted
him and dragged to his death. Not
content, the infurated mob carried
his body to the river and there in
the presence of a throng of thou
sands of wildly people, the
negro's body was excited incinerated.
