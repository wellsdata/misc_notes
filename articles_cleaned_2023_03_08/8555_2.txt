Holiday at Bonham, Tex.
DENISON, Tex., April 23.- Samuel
Massey and James Burke, negroes,
were hanged at Bonham, twenty miles
east of this city, at 3 o'clock yesterday
afternoon. Both men were confined in
the same cage and indulged in a fist
fight several hours before the execution
. They were taken from jail at 2
o'clock and spent an hour on the seaf
fold praying, singing and talking to
the crowd. Both confessed their crimes.
The scaffold was built in a forty- acre
field, arranged for both men to fall at
once Fully 15,000 people witnessed the
execution..
