Lynching Feared.
Centerville, Iowa, Feb. 22.- Gordon
Raney, living near Queen City, Mo.,
shot and killed his wife Friday night.
Raney escaped and a posse of citizens
followed with bloodhounds, but have
far failed to capture him. If he is
so captured he may be lynched.
