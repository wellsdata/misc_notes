MOB LYNCHES A NEGRO
WHO HAD ATTACKED WOMAN.
Anniston, Ala., Nov. 24.- A mob.
lynched Roy Rolston, a negro here
today for attacking Mrs. W. C. Cheat-
wood, wife of a white farmer. The
negro fled after his crime and was
tracked many miles. His pusuers
riddled him with shot and his body
then was burned. His victim is in
a critical condition and
may die ..
There is much feeling against
residents as a result of the affair negro.
