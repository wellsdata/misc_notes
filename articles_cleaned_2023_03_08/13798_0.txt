An Alleged Outrager Lynched.
DETROIT, June 15.- A man named Warner,
believed to be the person who outraged little
Nettie Lyons at Checoygan Tuesday night,
was taken from the jail at that place fast night
by a mob and hung. The girl identified him
as her assailant, but he maintained his inno
, even after being once let down to see if
cence a confession could be extorted from him.
