Negro Lynched For
Striking White Girl
ATHENS, Ga., Sept. 2.- Jim Reed Long,
negro, who was arrested early today for
striking Miss Violet Wood, 19 year old
daughter of Rev. John H. Wood of Rome,
over the head with an iron bar when she
screamed on finding him in the act of
burglarizing the house of her aunt, Miss
Pearl Saunders of Winder, Ga., whom
she was visiting, was taken from Sheriff
Camp. just out of Winder, late today by
mob, strung up on the limb of a pine
a tree and lynched.
