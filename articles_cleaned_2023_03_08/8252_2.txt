Rushed to the Hospital
Tallulah, La., July 4- When Conductor
Roberts, of the Iron Mountain Rail
road, demanded fare of Enos Stetson,
a negro, near here, this afternoon, the
latter shot Roberts down. The conductor
is probably mortally wounded.
He was rushed to the railroad hospital
at Magee, Ark. Stetson jumped from
the train and he is being searched for
by a large crowd of armed citizens to
night That he will be lynched if captured
., seems certain.
