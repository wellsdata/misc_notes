It is said that the mob which hung Tom
Blair at Mt. Sterling consisted of fifteen
persons, and the Governor has offered
$ 400 each for their apprehension and
arrest, and to this the county has added
$ 100 each. This reward has brought
three detectives on the ground, and in
less than a week one in
will likely be arrested, and the Grand
Jury, which meets next Monday, will
have no trouble indicting..
