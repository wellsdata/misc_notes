MORE LAW BREAKING- We are informed, from good
authority, that two or three persons were seized in Mays.
ville, two or three days ago, implicated in the abduction
of some negroes, and were passed through the ordeal of
( the now omnipotent) Judge Lynch, to the great gratification
of the citizens. Our informant states that the of
fenders were most shockingly whipped
[ Cincinnati. Whig, of 19th.
