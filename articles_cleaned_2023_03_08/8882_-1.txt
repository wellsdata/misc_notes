PERU NAMES DELEGATES.
And So Removes Doubts as to Conference
of American States.
Washington, Sept. 3.- All doubt
concerning the holding of an international
conference of American states
in the City of Mexico was removed to
day when announcement was made
that Peru had appointed three of her
most eminent men as delegates to the
conference.
According to advices sent the State
Department by Minister Dudley, Peru
has named Senor Alzamora, vise pres
ident of the Peruvian republic; Minis
ter Calderon and Senor Elmore, chief
justice of the Supreme Court of Peru
as her delegates.,
