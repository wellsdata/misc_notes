THE Rebel leaders in Little Rock went
out into the insurrectionary districts and advised
their followers that open war would not
do yet; that it would arouse the North, and
secure the election of Grant.
As a conse-
quence the Rebel battalions have left the
field and now confine themselves to private
assassinations and occasional highway robbery
of prominent Republicans, in a quiet
way, while Lynch law is executed against
colored men and women after the style
of the ancient regime. old
