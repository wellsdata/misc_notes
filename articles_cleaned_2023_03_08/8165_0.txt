- Four negroes, two men and two women,
were arrested and lodged in jail at Belgreen,
Ala, charged with burning the town of
Russellville. The women confessed and told
the whole story. A mob of two hundred and
fifiy armed men went to Belgreen, over
powered Jailer Waltrep and took Jeft Deus
mor and El Hudson, the two men, to a
neighboring white oak tree, swung them to a
limb filled them full of bullets and left them.
They, denied their guilt.
