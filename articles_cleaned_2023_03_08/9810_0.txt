Sheriff Overpowered;
Mob Lynches Negro
RICHMOND, Va., Aug. 3.- The sheriff
of Mecklenburg County was overpowered
early today while taking an unidentided
negro charged with the murder of Post
master Elmore, at Tobacco, Va., Monday
night, to jail. The crowd lynched the
negro and rode away without molesting
any one. A second negro involved in
the murder else escaped and is being pursued.
