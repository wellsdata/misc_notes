The Wisconsin Murderer Lynched and Hung by
the
Milwaukee, August 8.
Judge Larabee held a special term of court
yesterday at West Bend, Washington Co ..
for the trial of Debear, the murderer. The
jury brought in a verdict of murder in the first
degreo.
The prisoner was remanded to jail to await
sentence. The Sheriff started for the jail with
the prisoner, surrounded by the military. A
rush was made by the mob, when the military
gare way, and Debear was struck to the ground
by a stone. The mob then fell upon him,
pounded and mangled him, tied a rope to his
heels, drew him through the streets for half a
mile, and hung him by the heels to the lime
of a tree He remained hanging lifeless up to
6 P. M.. yesterday.
