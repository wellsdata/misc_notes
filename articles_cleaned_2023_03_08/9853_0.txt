HANG KNIFE- WIELDING NEGRO.
Mob Overpowers Guards and Applies
Lynch Law.
Lake Charles, La., Dec. 10.- Sauldren
Poydras, the negro who cut Chief
Deputy Sheriff C. M. Richaras
and
wife seriously with a razor Thursday,
was arrested near Walsh, 20 miles
east, and brought here yesterday. Officers
were about to lodge him in
prison when a mob overpowered the
guards, took Poydras and hanged him
to an electric light near the
court house. wire
