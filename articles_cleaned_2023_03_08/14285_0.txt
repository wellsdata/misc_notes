NEGRO LYNCHING PREVENTED.
A Murderer Transferred From Tecum
seh to Oklahoma City Jail.
Shawnee, Okla., Sept. 27.- John Anderson
, the negro who killed David
Eastes, white, by cutting his throat
with a razor, has been placed in the
Oklahoma city jail to prevent lynching
. A lynching party was being organized
to go to Tecumseh, but the
officers were notified and Anderson
was taken across the country in a
carriage to Norman, and thence
to closed Oklahoma City.
