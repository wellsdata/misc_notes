RECENT Hermosillo( Mex.) advices
report ninety- three people killed in that
State since the Apache outbreak, of which
twenty- seven were Americans. It was believed
many had been killed who had not
been reported. At Palmo ranch ten men
were killed lately. Two women were hung
up by the hands and frightfully mutilated.
The feeling in Southern Arizona was grow
ing more intense daily, and the number of
Rangers was increasing near San
Carlos, the objective rapidly point.
