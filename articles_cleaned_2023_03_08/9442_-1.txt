A CIRCULAR letter has been sent out
from St. Louis by a national executive
committee of colored citizens asking the
colored people of the United States to
have May 31 observed by them as a day
of general fasting and prayer. They
are asked to meet at the churches on
that day and pray for relief from
ent conditions, and for a lessening of
the lynching of negroes by whites that
are so frequent.
