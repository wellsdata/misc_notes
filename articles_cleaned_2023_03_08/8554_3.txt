Ohio White Caps.
COLUMBUS, O., Dec. 17.- The White Cap
organizations in this state have all been act
ing under charters taken out from the office
of the secretary of state under a variety of
names, such as" Brothers of Industry" and
" Knights of Liberty and Law." Governor
Foraker has issued a brief proclamation an
nulling all these charters, and saying that in
case of any further terrorizing, whipping or
threatening the members of the recent organizations
in the localities where the violence
occurs will be held responsible and proceeded
against accordingly. Governor Foraker told
a correspondent that he believed that out
rages would now cease. all
