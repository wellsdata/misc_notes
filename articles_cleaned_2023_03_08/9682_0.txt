THE NEGRO PROMPTLY LYNCHED.
Atlanta, Ga., Oct. 15.- Miss Blanche
Gray, a young lady of family, was go
ing from a friend's house to her sister's
, in the northern part of Spalding
county, late yesterday when a negro
slipped up behind her and assaulted
her in a
manner. About 1
' clock this morning the negro, Henry
Milner, was captured and taken to
Griffin. While he was in the hands of
a physician, a crowd took him to a
grove, where he was hung and shot to
death. A placard was attached to his
dead person:" we defend our wo
men from outrage Thus."
