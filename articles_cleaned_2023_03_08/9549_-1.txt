OUTLAW AND OFFICER SHOT.
Bloody Fight with Desperadoes in Tennessee
.
KNOXVILLE, Tenn., Jan. 26.- Yester
day in Claiborne County, near Cun.ber
Deputies Thompson and
land Gap,
Williams attempted to arrest two out
laws. A desperate running fight occurred
, and one of the outlaws was
shot. After he fell he took deliberate
aim and shot Deputy Thompson
through the heart. The other out
law was then captured, along with the
fellow who had been wounded. The
body of the dead deputy and two prisoners
were taken to Cumberland Gap.
It was reported here at midnight last
night that mob was about to lynch the
men under a arrest.
