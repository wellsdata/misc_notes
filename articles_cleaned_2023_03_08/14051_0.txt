Sandersville, Ga., Sept. 28.- Jim
Johnson, negro, was lynched near
here today for an assault alleged to
have been committed on a white
woman.
The negro was being taken froni
Sandersville to Wrightsville for
trial when the automobile bearing
him and an escort of officers was
stopped and Johnson taken from the
car by a mob of fifty persons, The
officers were disarmed and Johnson
taken to a bridge where he was
hanged. He is said to have
