The Lynch Law.
A special session of Judge Lynch's Court
was is lately held at Clinton, la. The ach
count states that a young man attacked a
little girl in the woods, and frightened her
by his lewd attempts. The girl's father
and about forty friends, took the fellow
across the river, and hung him by the hands
to limb of a tree, and gave him one
hundred lashes with hickory withes, raising
blisters on him, besides drawing
large blood, and then bade him travel.
