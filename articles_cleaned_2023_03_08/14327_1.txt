COLORED BOYS LYNCHED.
It Was Said That They Murdered a
Printer Named Hart.
Shelbyville, October 2.- Jimbo Fields
and Clarence Garrett, 16 and 18 years
respectively and colored, were lynched
early this morning for the alleged mur
der of Will C. Hart, a printer. Hart
was stoned to death on Saturday
night, September 21. The boys were
taken from the jail and hung from the
Chesapeake and Ohio tracks five hundred
yards from the jail. The jailer
refused to open the doors when the
lynchers appeared, but they were
tered down by the mob. bat
