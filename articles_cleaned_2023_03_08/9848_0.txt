AROUSED, ALA. BRIGHTON,
NEGRO SUSPECT LYNCHED:
BIRMINGHAM, Ala., Aug. 5.- Just as
a mass meeting was being held at
Brighton, ten miles south of Birmingham
, near the Woodward mines last
night, deputies brought in Will Miller,
a negro charged with connection in the
dynamiting of the house of Finley
Fuller, the night before last.
Today it was discovered that the lit
the calaboose had been broken into
and the body of Miller was found in
the woods hanging to a tree. He had
been lynched.
greatest excitement prevails
around The Brighton.
