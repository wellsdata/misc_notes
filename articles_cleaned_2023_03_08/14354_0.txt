SWIFT JUSTICE.
A Negro Murderer Taken From the
Authorities and Lynched.
MIDDLESBOROUGH( Ky.), March 26.
At Cumberland Gap, Tenn., last evening
J. A. Burke, a telegraph operator, and
Tom Hunter( colored) had a row over
some trifling matter. The negro left, and
procuring a shotgun, laid in ambush for
Burke, and when the latter came along
shot and killed him. The negro was captured
after a long chase. This evening
Hunter was forcibly taken from
authorities by a mob and lynched. the
