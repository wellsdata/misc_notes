NEGRO LYNCHED
Carolina Negro Taken Away From
the Officers.
CHARLOTTE, N. C., Aug. 29.
Avery Mills, colored, was banged and
riddled with bullets by a mob yester
day for the murder of M. H. Flock, ex
member of the legislature.
Officers were taking the negro to
jail when the mob overtook them,
took him away, and soon had him
strung up.
