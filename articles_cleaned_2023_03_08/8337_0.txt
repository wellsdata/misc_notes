A LYNCHING IN VIEW
A White Boy Shot and Killed by a
Negro Near Leaksville.
Charlotte, N. C., January 2.- A special
to The Observer from Leaksville, N. C ..
says:
Tom Rainey, a white boy, aged 12
years, was instantly kilied today by Ed.
Pettus, a negro, 17 years old. Rainey
met the negro in a field and the two
got into a quarrel when Pettus drew
a revolver and shot Rainey in the head.
escaped. There is danger of a
lynching Pettus if the negro is caught.
