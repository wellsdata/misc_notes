ONE IS LYNCHED
CENTRALIA, Wash., Nov. 11 .-( P) -A mob com
posed of many of the leading citizens of this town tonight
lynched one of the members of the I. W. W. gang which
killed three ex- soldiers by firing into a contingent of
former service men as it marched down one of the main
streets in a parade. The man secured from jail by the
mob was taken in an auto to a high bridge near here,
a rope placed about his neck, and he was dropped over
the guard rail. In addition to the three men already
dead, two other former soldiers are not expected to
thru the night. live
