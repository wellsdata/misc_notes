Negro Rapist Lynched.
Camden, Ark., July 31.- Lee Newton,
a negro, has been lynched by a mob
in the Cornie bottoms, 25 miles from
Magnolia. A month ago the negro was
found in the room of two young daughters
of a prominent family. Twice he
escaped from custody. He was being
taken to Magnolia officers when the
mob seized him. by
