Wounded Lyncher Dead.
NASHVILLE, May 3.- Charles Rear,
who was shot during the attack made
by the mob on the jail Friday night in
the effort to lynch Eph Grizzard, the
negro who assaulted the Misses Bruce
at Goodlettsville, is dead. Judge Ridley
, of the criminal court, has instruct
ed the grand jury that every member
of the mob was guilty of murder and
should be indicted. He appealed to the
to do its duty in bringing the
jury lynchers to justice.
