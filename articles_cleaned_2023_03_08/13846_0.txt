MOB HANGS A NEGRO.
Charged With Attempting to Enter
Woman's Room.
LOUISVILLE, Miss., June 4.- Essic
Bostic, a negro, has been hanged in the
woods near here by 20 band of forty
masked and armed men. The negro, in
the custody of a number of officers, was
being taken to Jackson for safe keeping.
The lynchers appeared and demanded the
prisoner, and Bostic was turned over to
the band.
Bostic was charged with having at
tempted to enter room of Mrs. Sarah
Gordon, a widow, the early Saturday morning.
