NEGRO LYNCHED BY
MOB IN MISSISSIPPI
Meridian, Miss., Nov. 25.- Morgan
Chambers, a negro, was taken from
Town Marshal Broadway and Deputy
Joe Camp at Meehan, twelve miles west
of here, tonight, by 200 masked men
and his body riddled with bullets.
The negro had earlier in the day
beaten and robbed Martin an
aged citizen of Point, a small station
two miles west of Meehan.
Dressler was taken to Meehan, where
he identified his assailant.

Broadway and Deputy Camp started
with the prisoner for the town prison.
