James Ray Gibson, a traveling sales
man, of Tucson, Ariz., was killed on
the Apache Trail near Roosevelt.
Star Daley, alias Ashmore and said to
be a deserter from the army, was ar
rested at Mesa on complaint of Mrs.
Gibson who told the officers that
Daley had killed her husband and then
attacked her. Daley was taken to
Phoenix and lodged in jail, later
taken by a mob of fifty and and hanged.
