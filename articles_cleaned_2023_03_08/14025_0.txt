GEORGIA NEGRO LYNCHED.
Shot to Death and the Body Burned at
the Stake.
Cedartown, Ga., Aug. 23.- Jim Glover
, a negro, was shot to death Monday
night near the home of the white girl
he had assaulted and his body dragged
a distance of about a mile into this
city and burned at the stake on the
courthouse square. A mob estimated
at 500 composed the shooting and
ing party. burn
