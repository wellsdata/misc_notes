PROBE STARTED
BY GRAND JURY
MANY INDICTMENTS RETURNED
IN CONNECTION WITH RACE
RIOTS IN OMAHA.
OMAHA, Neb., Nov. 19 .-( P) -The
grand judy which has been probing
the recent race riots in this city, in
which one negro was lynched and
the city jail and courthouse partially
Furned by mobs and the mayor seriously
injured as a result of his stand
against the rioters, has returned many
indictments implicating a considerable
number of people in the riots.
It is maintained by the report of the
grand jury that the trouble was caus
ed by crimes committed by negroes
against women and to undue
criticism white of public officials.
