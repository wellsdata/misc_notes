SHORT ROPE FOR TRICE.
Olive Branch, Moss, Negro Who Killed
Mistress Lynched by Mob.
Olive Branch, Miss., July 22. Andrew
Trice, a negro, who Saturday night
smashed the head of Susie Williams, his
mistress, while the woman lay sleeping,
was taken from officers near here to
day by a mob of 30 negros and hanged
Trice's body was thrown into a creek
near the spot the negro had cast
the corpse of where the woman.
