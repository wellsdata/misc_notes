GENERAL NEWS.
A mob lynched two men at Glencoe,
Minn, on Saturday. They had killed a
farmer and were pursued by Sheriff
Rogers and a deputy. The men resisted
arrest and the sheriff was shot They
had their trial last week and were found
guilty of murder in the second degree.
This did not satisfy the people so they
went to the jail, overpowered the guards
and lynched the two men.
A steam yacht containing 12 people
swamped in Niagara river on Sunday
was last and two people were drowned.
