APT TO BE LYNCHED.
Two Negroes Waylay an Aged Couple
and Assault the Woman.
RALEIGH, N. C., July 24.- News reaches
here of a horrible affair which occurred
yesterday in Northampton county. While
an aged white couple were traveling along
the road toward Margaretsville, they were
overtaken and attacked by two negroes,
who held the husband and committed a
criminal assault upon the woman. Both
were captured and lodged jail, and it is
reported that lynohing is openly in talked of,
