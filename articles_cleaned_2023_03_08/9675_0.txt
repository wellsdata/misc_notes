HELD COURT BY INCENDIARY FIRE
Judge Saves from Lynching Negro Charged
with Destroying Barn.
Norfolk, Va., Dec. 20.- To save Charles Bynum,
a negro, from lynching, Justice Morris of Driver,
Va., held court at 3 a. m. by the light of the fire
the negro is accused of having started. Bynum is
charged with having burned the barn of John L.
Bond. Several fine horses and a number of vehicles
and farm implements, together with several thou
pounds of cotton, were destroyed. The negro
sand was held for court.
