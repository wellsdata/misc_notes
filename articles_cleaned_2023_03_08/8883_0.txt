NEW MADRID, Mo., March 20 .-- The fire
at Tiptonville, Tenn., is said to have been
incendiary and is supposed to have been
started by negroes out of revenge for the
lynching of Ike Fitzgerald, colored, on
Saturday. At his trial the jury stood eleven
for hanging and one for life imprisonment.
That evening as the sheriff was taking
Fitzgerald to the jail a mob of 200 men
took the prisoner from the sheriff and
hanged him to a tree.
Before he was hanged the negro was tortured
in a horrible manner. The negroes of
Tiptonville and vicinity were greatly
wrought up over the mutilation and
