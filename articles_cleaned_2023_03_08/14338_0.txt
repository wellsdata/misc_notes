A Negro Murderer Lynched.
BRYAN( Texas), Nov. 18.- Last night
at the Allen farm, near here, while
gambling for pecans, a negro named
General Chatham was stabbed twice
and killed. Another negro, Tom Sweat,
was arrested for the crime, and while
being conveyed to Millican by a posse,
was taken from his guards and strung
