FOUR MEN ARE LYNCHED.
Two Murderers and Two Rapists
Executed by Arkansas Mobs.
Four colored men have been lynched in
the vicinity of Bearden, Ark., within the
last few days. Two were rapist and two
murders.
The ravishers, Devo
and
Huntly, were swung up three miles north
of Bearden, and the murderers met their
fate a few miles south of Bearden. The
work was done by two separate mobs, and
according reports created little excite-
