THINK NEGRO IS
WANTED FOR MURDER.
Anderson Lindsay, a negro arrest
ed early yesterday morning for beating
a woman, is said to be a murderer
and the police are holding him
until the matter can be investigated
.
Lindsay was arrested by Patrolman
Farrell for beating Lulla Ceasar at
4 o'clock in the morning. After the
arrest was made it was reported to
the police that Lindsay was wanted
for murder. No further in
somewhere formation has yet been secured.
