A Wife Murderer Lynched.
An armed body of one hundred and
fifty whites and thirty colored men rode
into Lafayette, La., broke open the par
ish jail and took therefrom Felix Keyes,
colored, who in a fit of jealousy, near
Carencro, foully murdered his wife, cut
ting her throat with a knife and then
knocking her in the head with an axe. He
was taken to the scene of his crime
executed by the mob. and
