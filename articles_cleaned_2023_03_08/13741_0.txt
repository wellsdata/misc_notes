LYNCHED. KENTUCKY NEGRO
Prisoner Accused of Attempting to En
ter Girl's Room.
Elkton, Ky., Oct. 13.- Shortly after
midnight a mob of 1,000, supposed to
be from Trenton, took Frank Leavell,
one- legged negro, from the jail here
a and lynched him in a nearby wood.
Leavell was arrested on the charge
of attempting to enter the room of a
lady at Trenton last Saturday
young night. He confessed.
