JERRY FLEMING
SEES DEAD BROTHER
Jerry Fleming, a brother of Sam
Fleming, the negro who was lynched
at MeGehee yesterday morning, and
who is a prisoner in the Jefferson
county jail, was given permission to
visit a local undertaking shop today
and view the body of his dead broth
er. At the request of John Young,
head waiter at the Hotel Jefferson,
Fleming was released by Sheriff Phil
pot long enough to make the visit.
He was then returned to the county
jail Fleming is in the county jail
awaiting. trial on a forgery charge.
