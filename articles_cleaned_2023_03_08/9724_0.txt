Negro Evades Lynching
Elkton, Ky, April 4.- Izon Taliaferro, a
negro, probably evaded lynching during
the night only through the pleading of
George Robinson, upon whose wife the ne
gro is alleged to have attempted a criminal
assault. Robinson, a prominent Todd
county farmer, begged that the law be
allowed to take its course, and the negro
was rushed to the county jail for
keeping. safe
