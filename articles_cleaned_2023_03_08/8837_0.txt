WOODLAWN, Wash., Oct. 12.- A young
man named Victor Beebe was killed Fri
day night by a man named Day, in a
quarrel about some cows which had destroyed
Beebe's corn. Day fled after the
shooting, but was captured next day
and a mob organized to lynch him. He
was taken to Kalama, but it is feared
