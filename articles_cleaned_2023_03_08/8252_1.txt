Two Negroes Killed
Lake Providence, La., July 4- Two
negroes were killed and a third wound
ed by H. Y. Evans, a white man, at
Enoka, about 19 miles from here late
today. It is reported that Evans brother
was being beaten by a negro bartender
when Evans opened fire, killing
the negro. A second negro who interfered
