CAIN IS GUILTY OF MURDER
Verdict of a Jury in a Texas Lynch
ing Case. Imposing Life
Sentence.
ST. LOUIS, Dec. 23.- A special to the
Post- Dispatch from Palestine, Tex., says:
The jury in the case of Ed Cain, charged
with conspiracy with the mob that lynched
the three Humphreys on the night of May
23, 1899, in Henderson county, returned a
verdict of guilty today. Cain's punishment
is fixed at life imprisonment in the penitentiary
. The case against the other mem
bers of the mob, who are charged directly
with the murder was adjourned till Wednesday
because of, the Christmas holiday.
