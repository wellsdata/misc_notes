AFRAID OF A MOB.
It Appears That There Is Still Danger
at Alma.
Although Sheriff Treu of Wabaunsee
county stoutly asserted that there was
no danger of mob violence toward
" Dick" Williams and James Render,
alias Denver Kid, during their stay in
Alma yet he changed his mind and
brought Render back to the county
jail in Topeka Friday night.
There has been talk enough in Alma
to warrant the sheriff in being cautious.

murder in Alma at the same time there
was danger of lynching. Render was
