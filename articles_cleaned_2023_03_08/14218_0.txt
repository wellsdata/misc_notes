A horrible murder and application of
Lynch law, occurred in Phillips county,
Ark., on the 9th ult. A Mr. Henry Yuly
was found murdered and buried between
two logs, about 600 yards from his own
house; and two of his slaves, who had run
away some time previous, having given
themselves up, were suspected. Those hav
ing them in charge being satisfied of their
guilt, tied them to trees and burnt them
