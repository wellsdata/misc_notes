A WHITE RIOT IN CANADA WEST.
NEGROES DRIVEN FROM THEIR QUARTERS.
OIL SPRINGS, C. W., March 16.- A serious
riot occurred on Saturday night between whites
and negrocs. The whites organized a force
and marched to the negro quartors, and, after
ordering them away, destroyed their property
and burned their houses.
The negroes fled to the woods.
Three of the rioters were captured, and
ral of them wounded. seve
