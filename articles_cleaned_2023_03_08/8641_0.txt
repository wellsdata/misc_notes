Two Negroes Lynched.
NASHVILLE, Tenn., March 31.- Daniel
Guthrie, a prominent citizen of Crockett
county, was murdered ten days ago by
Weakly Ridely and Tobe Williams, two no
groes, who were arrested and lodged in jail
at Alamo. The crime caused great indignation
in the community and a crowd of men
took the negroes out and, lynched them.
