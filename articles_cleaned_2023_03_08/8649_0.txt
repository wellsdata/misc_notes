Investigating the Lynching.
MEMPHIS, Tenn., Sept 6- The investigation
into the lynching of six negroes at
Millington last week is being pushed with
vigor and as result the grand jury yester
day found five indictments. More are expected
to- day. The indicted men include
Detective W. F. Richardson who led the
negroes into the hands of the mob, A. At
kinson, the man who drove the wagon on
the night of the lynching and Jeff Laxter,
merchant at Kerrville, be
a lieved to have been the leader of the mob.
