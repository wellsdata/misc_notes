NEGRO IS SAVED FROM
LYNCHING BY TIMELY
ARRIVAL OF THE COPS
( By The Associated Press.)
Des Moines, Ia., July 3.- Timely
arrival of the police saved Samuel
Lee, a negro, from lynching at the
hands of a mob of several hundred
people here tonight. The crowd
gathered after Lee had stabbed
and probably fatally injured his
wife, at the entrance of a local
amusement park.
Lee attempted to escape down a
struck and
side street, but was
knocked down by a passing auto
mobile truck. The crowd that
gathered quickly took up the
to lynch him. A rope was obtained
and the negro was being led to a
nearby tree when a of police
rescued him. squad
